I am an polyvalent ecologist based in the french Pyrenees. I have a background in academia, having specialized in animal behavior research, before switching to a more applied side of ecology, working as an environmental engineer for consulting firms or governmental agencies. Since 2025, I have started my own independant practice, and am now proposing my abilites for the benefit of research or conservation projects. My varied experiences have allowed me to acquire a very broad set of skills, and I can assist at any stage of ecology projects, including litterature review, conception of experimental designs, collection of data in the field, data management and analyses, development of interactive applications for outreach, report writing, and so on. Please check out my CV or the other pages for more detailed information, and do not hesitate to get in touch!
