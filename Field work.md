## What I can do in the field
---  

<img class="left-img" src="https://baptisteaverly.github.io/img/DSC_1407.JPG" >

**Before the field**  
Good preparation can prevent many hurdles further down the road. Because I have been through the hurdles, I can assist during the early stages of your project to make sure the data collection phase goes as smoothly as possible. The diverse projects I have been involved in from start to finish have given me the tools and experience to help you with various aspects of scientific planning, such as:
- literature review
- conception of experimental designs and sampling plans tailored to your research question
- drafting of detailed field protocols 
- construction and/or preparation of specialized field equipment (field cameras, Automated Recording Units, custom animal collars with various sensors, artificial feeders...)

**Data collection**  
Tireless in the field, I enjoy working outdoors in all types of terrain and various meteorological conditions: the French Pyrenees mountains, the Kalahari Desert or the Canadian boreal forest are some examples of environments in which I have enthusiastically worked in the past. Since I am used to dealing with ecological datasets (see [Data work](https://baptisteaverly.github.io/Data%20work)), I know the importance of carefully collected data and will carry out work in the field with high standards of rigor and precision. Here are some examples of what I can perform for your project:
- installation and maintenance of specialized field equipment
- ecological inventories of flora and/or fauna
- behavioral observations
- capture and handling of birds and small mammals, morphometric measurements

**Outreach**  
I am a firm believer in the importance of knowledge sharing and transmission for the benefit of all parties involved: across disciplines, across levels of expertise, across profession types, and across ages. With experience in outreach and popular education, I can help to get the outcome of your project across to a variety of publics. Depending on your goal, this can be through the creation of educational content such as games or flyers, the development of interactive online tools based on your results (see [Data work](https://baptisteaverly.github.io/Data%20work)), or the organization and conduct of outreach events (conferences, information booths...).
